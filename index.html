+ Current Work +

I currently work with Dr. Lao and Mr. Clements at Clemson University on a project about human pose estimation with transformer models & deep neural nets.

I am the founder and executive director of ARQuest Student Science & Engineering Research Network (ARQuest SSERN for short).

As the lengthy name might suggest, it’s an awesome community of 200+ scientifically driven high school students from all over the world who gather together to share our scientific journeys/ideas/resources, work on research projects, craft articles, etc… Join us now!

Within ARQuest SSERN, I oversee current projects such as our summer research program to make sure that our researchers get all the help they need and can learn the most possible. I also work closely with all of the departments to map out our visions and strategize procedures to turn them into a reality. Our team is working hard this summer to plan even funner events, create even better opportunities, and develop resources :)

Within the summer research program, I am also the research mentor for group b, so I closely interface with the team through literature review, designing, coding, testing an optimized ribs segmentation to detect/classify fractures based on ct scans as input.
