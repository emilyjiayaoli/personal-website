Hi! I am Emily, a 16 year old from Irvine, CA, who is passionate about computer science, AI/ML research (specifically computer vision w/ deep learning atm), dancing, and singing. I am just getting into ML tech start ups and 3D printing.


+ Current Work +

I currently work with Dr. Lao and Mr. Clements at Clemson University on human pose estimation and prediction with deep neural nets.

I am the founder and executive director of ARQuest Student Science & Engineering Research Network (ARQuest SSERN for short). As the lengthy name suggests, it is a community of 200+ scientifically driven high school students from all over the world that connect through sharing resources, working on research projects, writing articles, etc… I oversee current events and projects such as our summer research progam to make sure that it runs smoothly and work closely with all of the departments to map out our visions and make them a reality. Our team is working hard this summer to plan even funner events, create even better opportunities, and develop resources :)

Within the summer research program, I am also the research mentor for group b, so I closely interface with the team through literture review, designing, coding, testing an optimized ribs segmentation to detect/classify fractures based on ct scans as input.
